# Tea Tax Background

Tea Tax is a free, AI-powered front door for tax filing. It helps people turn messy tax inputs into a structured tax situation, then compares filing options with transparent pricing and fit so they can choose with confidence.

## What Tea Tax Is

- A consumer-first tax filing aggregator, not another tax prep provider.
- A guided intake experience that works for unprepared users.
- A comparison layer across filing options (software, free options, and professionals).
- A portability layer so users can take their tax situation anywhere.

## Core Problem It Solves

Most taxpayers face opaque pricing, confusing choices, and a stressful intake process. Tea Tax is built to reduce that confusion, expose true costs, and help users make an informed filing decision quickly.

## V1 Focus

- Intake Engine (collect and structure tax situation data).
- Comparison Engine (transparent recommendation and routing).
- Strong privacy constraints as a product requirement, not an afterthought.
